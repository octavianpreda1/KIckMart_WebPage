NIKE

Nike is a global sportswear and footwear company that offers a range of athletic and casual products for men, women, and kids. Founded in 1964, Nike has become a household name known for its innovative designs, quality materials, and focus on performance. The brand is popular among athletes and fitness enthusiasts, and offers a wide range of products including sneakers, apparel, and accessories for a variety of sports and activities. Nike is recognized worldwide for its iconic "swoosh" logo and slogan "Just Do It," and has a strong reputation for promoting sustainability and social responsibility in its manufacturing and business practices.

Jordan

Jordan is a subsidiary brand of Nike, named after former basketball player Michael Jordan. The brand specializes in high-performance athletic footwear, apparel and accessories for basketball and lifestyle, and is known for its innovative designs and limited-edition releases. Jordan is recognized worldwide for its iconic "Jumpman" logo and is a favorite among basketball enthusiasts and collectors.

Puma

Puma is a German sportswear brand that designs and produces a wide range of athletic and lifestyle products. Founded in 1948, Puma is known for its innovative designs and use of technology to enhance performance. The brand offers products for sports such as soccer, running, golf and motorsport, as well as fashion and lifestyle products. Puma is recognized for its iconic cat logo and collaborations with well-known designers and celebrities. Puma is also committed to sustainability and ethical business practices.
